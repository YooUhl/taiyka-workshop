# Cover/Thumbnail Design Brief

For Canva / designer if you want polished raster variants beyond the SVG I shipped.

## Files needed
1. **Square cover** 1200×1200 (Gumroad listing, IG post, Skool resource thumb)
2. **Landscape banner** 1600×900 (LinkedIn post, FB share, Open Graph)
3. **Story/Reel cover** 1080×1920 (IG/TikTok promo)

## Brand
- Background: gradient from `#0A1628` (top) → `#000000` (bottom) + radial blue glow centered upper-third
- Accent: gradient `#00A6FF → #00E5FF` (Taiyka electric blue → cyan)
- Text white `#FFFFFF`, secondary `#8DA2C0`
- Font: Inter (or anything geometric sans — Satoshi, Manrope, Plus Jakarta also fine)

## Visual hierarchy
1. Massive **"5"** in accent gradient (the hook)
2. **"workflows n8n"** — bold white, just below the 5
3. **"pour entrepreneurs IA"** (or EN: "for AI entrepreneurs") — muted, smaller
4. **5 chips** at the bottom listing each workflow with emoji
5. **TAIYKA** logo + `@manu_ai.to` at the very bottom

## Vibe
Dark, techy, electric. Think: SaaS hero meets crypto poster. Confident, not cluttered. The "5" should feel like a punch.
